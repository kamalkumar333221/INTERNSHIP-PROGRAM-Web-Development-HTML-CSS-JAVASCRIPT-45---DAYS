Abount of Google :-

Google, a global technology leader, continues to shape the digital landscape by committing to innovation and information accessibility. Google's groundbreaking search engine has expanded into a vast ecosystem of products and services, including Gmail, Google Maps, YouTube, and the Android operating system. Driven by its core mission to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful, Google has significantly influenced how people connect, learn, work, and interact with the world. Technologies like AI and cloud computing are developed by the company. Products like Google Assistant and Google Cloud underscore Google's impact on various industries and its commitment to improving daily life.
